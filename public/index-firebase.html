About Me

My name is Ryan Pelletier, I currently work as a software engineer at Booz Allen Hamilton. My main areas of expertise include Spring MVC development with Java, as well as using Angular 1.X and Bootstrap on the front end.

I started my development career at a small startup called SharpTop Software. I worked on Grails web applications. It was hard, but I learned a lot! SharpTop wrote a nice article about me.

Education
